Our Mission Is to Connect Brilliance with Opportunity

Andela makes it possible for digital talent to solve complex and compelling problems, changing their career trajectory and quality of life. Andela clients craft a workforce that reflects the world around them –– with greater speed and more agility.

Learn MoreBook a discovery call

Brilliance is evenly distributed,
opportunity is not

Greater opportunity creates greater prosperity and success for both your business and the technologists who support it. That’s why we connect brilliant talent from around the world with opportunities to solve meaningful challenges at exciting companies.
Everyone wins.

Andela is changing the way the world works

We’ve built a thriving global marketplace, powered by Andela Talent Cloud. It allows businesses and technologists to co-create a healthier, more sustainable, more productive way of working.

Andela clients get access to world-class talent from across the globe, particularly in untapped and overlooked hiring markets.

Andela talent gets access to vast resources for upskilling, education, networking, and of course, the best job opportunities.

We envision a borderless world where the right person is chosen to do the job. Where brilliant people in untapped markets establish meaningful relationships with the world’s best brands.

Anyone can put bodies in seats — but that’s not what Andela is about. We're about making the way the world works work better, for everyone.

Andela combines proven solutions with breakthrough technologies to find the right talent for the right role at the right speed.
